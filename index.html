What is PromptX & DataX?

PromptX & DataX 2026 is a two-day Creative Intelligence & Emerging Technology Festival hosted by CiPHER at the University of Southampton Delhi Campus.

The event brings together students, innovators, and analytical thinkers to tackle real-world challenges in Artificial Intelligence, digital innovation, and data-driven decision making.

Through competitions, hackathons, and expert discussions, participants explore how modern technology can be applied responsibly and creatively in industry environments.

About the University

The University of Southampton, founded in 1862, is one of the United Kingdom’s leading research universities and a member of the prestigious Russell Group — representing the UK's most research-intensive institutions.

Southampton is internationally recognised for pioneering work in Artificial Intelligence, Computer Science, Engineering, Data Science, and Digital Innovation. Its research drives breakthroughs across industries ranging from robotics and cybersecurity to healthcare, aerospace, and sustainable technologies.

Through global partnerships, cutting-edge laboratories, and a strong culture of interdisciplinary collaboration, the university continues to shape the future of technology and innovation.

Event Competitions

Prompt Hacking Challenge

Step into the role of an AI security researcher in this high-intensity Red Team vs Blue Team challenge. Participants design AI system prompts, defensive guardrails, and clever exploits to test the limits of modern language models.

Expect strategic thinking, creative attacks, and real-world AI safety scenarios that mirror how companies test and secure intelligent systems.

No-Code Hackathon

Build a startup-style product in hours — not weeks. Teams will create functional digital solutions using modern no-code platforms and AI-assisted tools.

Focus on product thinking, automation, and problem-solving while turning creative ideas into real prototypes without writing traditional code.

Data Analysis Challenge

Turn raw data into meaningful insight. Participants will explore real-world datasets, identify patterns, and present compelling data-driven stories using modern visualization techniques.

Success requires analytical thinking, clarity of communication, and the ability to transform complex data into actionable conclusions.

AI Conference Panel

A curated discussion featuring researchers, technologists, and industry leaders exploring the evolving impact of artificial intelligence across society.

Topics range from AI in business and cybersecurity to ethics, governance, and the future of human-AI collaboration.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can participate?
Students from schools, universities and colleges across India are welcome to participate in PromptX & DataX 2026.
Is there a registration fee?
No. Participation in PromptX & DataX 2026 is completely free.
How do I register for the event?
You can register for PromptX & DataX directly using the Register button available on this website. Clicking the button will open the registration form. Alternatively, you can also register using this link:

https://forms.office.com/e/CFcCwwwsAx
When is the registration deadline?
The final registration deadline for PromptX & DataX is 17 March 2026. Teams will receive confirmation and event details through email after registrations close.
What should I bring to the event?
Participants should bring a laptop, charger, and any tools they may need for competitions or presentations along with waterbottles. Internet access and workspace will be provided at the venue.
Where will the event be hosted?
PromptX & DataX 2026 will be hosted at the University of Southampton Delhi Campus, located in International Tech Park Gurgaon, near Sector 59 on Golf Course Extension Road, Gurugram. The campus provides modern facilities designed for technology events, research collaboration, and innovation.
How do I reach the venue?
The event will take place at the University of Southampton Delhi Campus in Gurugram. The nearest metro station is Sector 55–56 (Rapid Metro Gurugram). Shuttle transportation will be arranged from the metro station to the campus for participants. Detailed pickup timings and instructions will be shared with registered participants before the event.
Do I need to have a team to participate?
Most competitions at PromptX & DataX are team-based. Participants are encouraged to register in teams of at least 3 members. If you do not have a team, you may still register individually and teams may be formed during the event depending on availability.
Do I need to attend both days of the event?
Yes. PromptX & DataX is designed as a two-day experience with different competitions and sessions scheduled across both days. Participants are strongly encouraged to attend the full event to take part in all activities.
What are the timings for the event?
The event will begin at approximately 8:00 AM on both days. Activities will continue through the day with breaks and will conclude in the evening around 5:30–6:00 PM. The detailed schedule is available in the agenda section above.
Will participants receive certificates?
Yes. All participants will receive e-certificates acknowledging their participation. Top performers and winners will additionally receive physical merit certificates during the closing ceremony.
Which students can participate?
The event is open to students from Grade 9–12 as well as first and second year college students. Anyone interested in technology, artificial intelligence, or data is welcome to participate.
What if I have no prior experience in AI?
No prior experience is required. The event includes guided sessions and mentors who will help participants understand the challenges and tools involved. You will be introduced to the concepts needed to participate effectively in the competitions.
Do I need prior AI or coding experience?
No prior experience is required, however some prior experience with AI tools would help. Some events are beginner-friendly while others allow advanced participants to explore deeper technical challenges.
Do I need to bring my own laptop?
Yes. Participants are encouraged to bring their own laptops as most challenges involve working with digital tools, datasets, and online platforms.
Will food be provided during the event?
Yes. Lunch and refreshments will be provided for participants on both days of the event.
How will teams be confirmed?
Confirmed participants and teams will receive official communication via email after the registration deadline.
